About the Reading Group

Welcome to the LLM Reading Group at Case Western Reserve University. This is a weekly gathering for researchers, students, and enthusiasts to discuss the latest developments in Large Language Models, their applications, and related research.

We encourage active participation from all members. If you would like to present a paper, share your research, or lead a discussion on a topic of interest, please submit a presentation proposal via pull request on our GitHub repository.

Want to Present?

We welcome presentations on:

  • Recent LLM research papers
  • Novel applications and use cases
  • Technical deep dives and tutorials
  • Ethical considerations and societal impacts
  • Your own research and projects

Check out our Contributing Guide to learn how to submit your presentation.
